These are the hardware projects I have taken on

Interacting with hardware is important to me, because I crave the connection to the physical world that it provides. While I enjoy working with software, I can't resist the excitement that comes with designing and builiding parts that will experience stress and strain and cycle-fatigue and vibrations. Where computers embody a world of logic and theory and mathematics, hardware exists in a realm where things go wrong unpredictably. Designing and testing against the challenges of such an environment stimulates me, and constitutes one of my favorite parts of engineering.
